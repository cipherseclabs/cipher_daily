Weekly Brief – July 14, 2025

Cybersecurity Events & Incidents

  • CitrixBleed 2 (CVE‑2025‑5777): Critical Citrix NetScaler ADC/Gateway flaw actively exploited in the wild. Affects multiple versions; threat actors can hijack sessions via memory over-read. Patching advised immediately. Source: https://www.techradar.com/pro/security/citrixbleed-2-exploits-are-now-in-the-wild-so-patch-now
  • Qantas call‑center breach: Data on ~5.7 million customers (names, emails, birthdates, frequent flyer & contact details) stolen. No payment or passport info compromised. Ongoing investigation; suspected link to Scattered Spider. Source: https://www.techradar.com/pro/security/qantas-confirms-5-7-million-customers-impacted-by-data-breach
  • Ingram Micro ransomware: Attack with SafePay strain disrupted global IT supply, operations delayed. Entry via third-party or unpatched system. Source: https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/week-cybersecurity-july-611-2025-surge-breaches-ransomware-7lidc
  • Azure Blob exposure: Misconfigured Azure container exposed ~26 million resumes from U.S. users, raising risk of phishing/scam campaigns. Source: https://www.itpro.com/security/data-breaches/26-million-cvs-were-exposed-when-a-recruiting-software-firm-left-a-misconfigured-azure-container-open-cybersecurity-experts-warn-its-an-easy-mistake-that-s-becoming-far-too-common
  • Pierce County Libraries breach: ~336 K individuals notified after "Inc group" ransomware exposed personal data. Source: https://social.cyware.com/category/breaches-and-incidents-news
