Weekly Brief – August 7 to August 13, 2025

Cybersecurity Events & Incidents

  • Federal judiciary system breach: U.S. federal court systems were breached, exposing sealed indictments and legal filings. Exploited flaws date back to 2020. Russian state-linked actors are suspected. Courts revert to paper filings amid overhaul efforts. Source: https://www.politico.com | Source: https://www.politico.com
  • Bouygues Telecom data breach: 6.4 million French customers' personal data exposed via a third-party Salesforce CRM breach. Includes IBANs, contracts, and IDs. CNIL and ANSSI notified. Source: https://www.itpro.com
  • PS1Bot malvertising campaign: New malware spreads via malvertising and Docker Hub, with capabilities for info-stealing, persistence, and lateral movement. Source: https://thehackernews.com
  • Fortinet VPN brute-force attacks: Over 780 IPs involved in global brute-force campaign targeting Fortinet SSL VPNs. Threat shifting toward FortiManager. Source: https://thehackernews.com
  • ShinyHunters & Scattered Spider: Collaboration observed in extortion campaigns using phishing, vishing, and VPN obfuscation. Source: https://thehackernews.com
